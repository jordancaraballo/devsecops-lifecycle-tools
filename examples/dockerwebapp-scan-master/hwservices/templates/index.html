Who We Are

We are a company that seeks to ensure customers satisfaction at all times.

Hot Water Company was created in 2018 for a Data Base Class Project. We are excited to bring new manufacturers, brands, and models for the amusement of our lovely customers. Here at HW-Company we leverage the best spa services using science and the art of creativity. You can be part of us in the following ways.

Manufacturer

As a manufacturer you will have the ability of creating and selling your own spa models. You are required to launch at least two brands every year, for up to twenty models.

Brand

You can become part of our Brand team responsible for creating exciting Brands for different purposes and places. Our brands are divided in models that serve as a guide towards total relaxation products.

Model

This team is responsible for creating and implementing amazing designs to the taste of the customer. We have an incredible number of default models, but customers can build their own.

Customer

You are more than welcome to join our big number of satisfied customers that experienced relaxation at another level. To communicate with anyone from the time go to the Contact Us section and enjoy the space of your lifetime.
